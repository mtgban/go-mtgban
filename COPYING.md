Open source projects should use the AGPLv3 license.

People who do not want or cannot abide by the AGPLv3 license can buy a commercial license
that will fund the project.

The commercial license gives all the source code, but allows to skip the AGPLv3 obligations,
and gives a license similar to a permissive license over the codebase.
